Hyperbolic Functions

Given the unit hyperbola x 2 - y 2 = 1 , the hyperbolic functions are defined by the coordinates of a point at which the area α 2 has been swept out.

The following formulae can be derived from it: sinh( α) = e α - e -α 2 and cosh( α) = e α + e -α 2
